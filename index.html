I am a Research Engineer at the Cybersecurity Lab in TCS Research, Pune. I did my MS by Research working under supervision of Prof. C.V. Jawahar and Prof. Anand Mishra on script and language identification in document images and scene images at IIIT Hyderabad. I also did internshipt under mentorship of Dr. Nataraj Jammalmadakka at Intel Labs, Bengaluru.

My current research interest spans Computer Vision, Adversarial Machine Learning. To be specific, my aim is to make the deep learning models more robust against the adversarial inputs.
